Open Source

  • Swiss Ephemeris: Haskell bindings to the C Swiss Ephemeris library, exposes functions and types useful for Horoscopy (I use it for freenatalchart.xyz to obtain planetary positions and calculate house cusps.)
  • Timezone Detect: Half Haskell bindings to the C Zone Detect library, half functions built on top of Time NG packages to provide the ability to detect a Timezone based on coordinates, and convert local time in a Unix timezone to UTC. (I also use this in freenatalchart.xyz , to not depend on third-party services for timezone detection.)
